According to the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), in 2020, there were 7,759 hate crime incidents reported by law enforcement agencies in the United States. This represents a 6% increase from the previous year. Of these incidents, 59.5% were motivated by race/ethnicity/ancestry bias, 20.4% were motivated by religious bias, and 14.2% were motivated by sexual orientation bias. The remaining incidents were motivated by gender identity, disability, and other biases.
